About Us

Welcome to Rock Solid Pet Grooming, where your dog's comfort is our top priority. We believe every pup deserves a stress-free grooming experience, which is why we have moved away from the high-volume assembly line model. We are a boutique space where belly rubs and positive reinforcement are just as important as the haircut.

With over 3 years of experience, our mission is to provide a calm, safe environment where your best friend is treated like family. From quiet drying techniques to careful styling, every appointment is handled with patience, detail, and genuine care.

Whether it is a quick nail trim or a full spa day, the goal is simple: your pup leaves happy, smelling great, and excited to come back next time.

Fully insured for pet grooming services.
